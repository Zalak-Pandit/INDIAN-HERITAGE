Discover the most engaging places

Hyderabad

A city of contrasts, Hyderabad exudes an old-world charm of its own with the Old City (Charminar side), Hitech City (Cyberabad) and the other areas lying between the old and the new.

Papikondalu

Papikondalu mountain range runs along the River Godavari (Western Godavari) and is located in Rajahmundry, Andhra Pradesh

Nagarjunsagar

Nagarjunasagar is a site of spectacular man-made marvel flanked by equally enchanting panoramic vistas of lush green surrounding.

Taste the flavours of goa

Sarva Pindi

Sarva Pindi is an excellent choice as it is cooked with at least one component of each stage of the food pyramid.

Malidalu

Fully packed with nutrients, it is the best solution for anyone craving for a sweet after a meal.

Sakinalu

It is mainly made during the festival of Sankranti or is offered during marriage.

Garijalu

Tamarind, coconut, and rice are very common ingredients used in the traditional food of Tamil Nadu, especially Tamil Brahmin food.

Handicrafts from the villages

Shawls

These are iconic and traditional, woven with intricate patterns that represent the various tribes. Each tribe has its unique designs and motifs.

Beadwork

Bead necklaces and jewelry are integral to Naga traditional attire,showcasing vibrant colors and intricate patterns.

Dimapur

Wood Carving: Naga artisans are skilled in wood carving, creating beautiful sculptures,masks, and other decorative items that often depict tribal myths and folklore.
